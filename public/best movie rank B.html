THE BEST MOVIE ACCORDING TO CLEOPATRA

My top movies of all time

A tribe called Judah

This 2023 movie is about a single mother with five sons from different fathers and tribes. It was produced by Funke Akindele and directed by Adeoluwa Owu.

Funmilayo Ransome

This 2024 biopic is about the life of Funmilayo Ransome-Kuti, a leader who fought against colonialism and patriarchy in Nigeria. It was directed by Bolanle Austen-Peters.

Lionheart

This movie was Netflix's first Nigerian original film. It stars and was directed by Genevieve Nnaji, who plays Adaeze, who runs the family business after her father becomes ill.
